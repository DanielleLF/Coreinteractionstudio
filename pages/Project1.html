Project 1

Research question

How can the food waste in developed countries be systematically reduced in today's household?

I am interested in this topic because with 40% of food supply going to waste each year in the USA, I think that there is an urgent need to educate the consumer about simple tips on how to reduce waste and take responsibility. Although this is not a new topic, food continues to be wasted in many households. In addition to the obvious economic impact, the repercussions of these actions seriously affect environmental issues like greenhouse gas emissions, and the need for larger landfills.

Ideation

With my application I hope to help people reduce food waste. By creating tools that aid and simplify the planning of meals and educate the consumer about how to right-size portions and shop smart, I envision regular users reducing their carbon footprint. The application will offer tools like scanning expiration dates and be reminded before the food goes bad. In addition, users will have options to look up recipes, plan their meals, have a calendar with expiration dates, shop smarter and record their waste to understand the monetary and environmental impact of food waste.
