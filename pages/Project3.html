Project 3

App Description

For Porject 3 I will design a movie app called Winner. The interaction part of the app will be when you are swiping through the cards horizontaly and the different taps. One tap on the card will flip the card and proved you with the synopsis of the movie. Another tap will take you back to the main movie image. If the user taps the card twice, the user will be taken to a new page with all the movie information.
